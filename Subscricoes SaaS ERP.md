# Subscricoes SaaS ERP

## Purpose

Subscricoes SaaS ERP is the commercial operating layer for selling [[NOVA-ERP]] itself as a SaaS product: plans, prices, add-ons, contracts, entitlements, recurring billing, usage limits, lifecycle and access enforcement.

## Role In NOVA-ERP

This module is different from customer ERP billing. It governs the platform business model: what each tenant can use, what they owe, when renewal happens, what happens during trial, upgrade, downgrade, overdue, suspension, cancellation or enterprise exception.

The core boundary: SaaS entitlements decide whether a tenant has access to a module/feature/limit. They do not replace [[Permissoes e Auditoria ERP]]. A user still needs tenant membership and action permission. Entitlement can deny a feature globally for the tenant; permission decides whether a specific user may perform an allowed action.

The target data model and invariants for this module are committed in [[2026-05-29 - Schema Decision - SaaS Subscriptions Billing and Entitlements]] (platform-owned catalog, tenant-scoped contracts, computed effective entitlements, platform-admin-write/tenant-read RLS split and treasury-derived payment status).

## Design Gates Before Implementation

- Tenant gate: define subscription ownership per tenant before plan access is enforced.
- Entitlement gate: distinguish plan entitlement from user permission and from feature flags.
- Billing gate: decide whether NOVA-ERP SaaS invoices are generated by the internal fiscal module, external billing provider or a platform billing record first.
- Access gate: define hard vs soft limits, grace periods and safe suspension behavior.
- Lifecycle gate: define trial, activation, renewal, past due, suspension, cancellation, expiry and reactivation transitions.
- Audit gate: every plan, price, entitlement, contract, billing and suspension change must be traceable.
- Fiscal gate: if NOVA-ERP invoices Cabo Verde customers for SaaS, confirm IVA/e-Fatura treatment before production billing.

## Core Workflows

- Define plan catalog, price matrix and lifecycle.
- Define add-ons and add-on prices.
- Define plan/add-on entitlements for modules, features, integrations and limits.
- Create a subscription contract for a tenant.
- Apply subscription-level entitlement overrides for approved enterprise exceptions.
- Capture immutable subscription snapshots on material changes.
- Run recurring billing cycles.
- Generate SaaS billing records or invoices.
- Track usage metrics and enforce limits.
- Detect overdue billing, apply grace period and suspend safely when needed.
- Reactivate tenants after payment or administrative resolution.
- Audit subscription and entitlement changes.

## Required Master Data

- Plan catalog.
- Price catalog by billing frequency and contract rule.
- Add-on catalog.
- Entitlement catalog: modules, features, limits and integration capabilities.
- Billing frequencies and periods.
- Payment terms and grace-period rules.
- Usage metric definitions.
- Suspension and reactivation policies.
- Enterprise exception approval rules.

## Candidate Domain Model

- `saas_plans`: commercial plan identity, name, status, market, lifecycle and display order.
- `saas_plan_prices`: price matrix by plan, billing frequency, currency, contract term and effective dates.
- `saas_plan_entitlements`: module/feature/limit/integration entitlements included in the plan.
- `saas_addons`: commercial extras beyond the base plan.
- `saas_addon_prices`: recurring or one-shot add-on pricing.
- `saas_addon_entitlements`: extra capabilities unlocked by add-ons.
- `saas_subscriptions`: tenant subscription contract header, lifecycle state, current period and billing settings.
- `saas_subscription_items`: contracted items such as base plan, add-ons, discounts and extras.
- `saas_subscription_entitlements`: approved tenant-specific overrides.
- `saas_subscription_snapshots`: immutable commercial snapshot for relevant changes.
- `saas_subscription_events`: lifecycle, billing, payment, enforcement and admin event history.
- `saas_billing_runs`: one billing execution for a subscription period.
- `saas_billing_run_lines`: detailed recurring billing lines.
- `saas_invoices`: customer-facing SaaS invoice/billing record, linked to financial evidence where applicable.
- `saas_usage_metrics`: periodic usage snapshots by tenant.

The source architecture keeps the `saas_*` namespace for compatibility while strengthening the domain model.

## Candidate State Machine

### Subscription Lifecycle

- `draft`: contract prepared but not active.
- `pending_activation`: awaiting activation conditions.
- `trialing`: tenant has trial access under explicit limits.
- `active`: subscription is current and access is normal.
- `past_due`: unpaid during grace period; access may be degraded but data remains intact.
- `suspended`: access restricted after grace expiry.
- `canceled`: subscription intentionally cancelled.
- `expired`: contract period ended without renewal.

Legacy UI compatibility may map `pending_activation` to `pending`, `trialing` to `trial` and `canceled` to `cancelled`, but the target domain vocabulary should keep the more precise states.

### Billing Run State

- `draft`: billing run created but not finalized.
- `calculated`: lines resolved from active subscription items.
- `issued`: billing record/invoice created.
- `settled`: payment evidence linked.
- `past_due`: payment overdue.
- `voided`: billing run invalidated through controlled process.

## Entitlement Boundary

Entitlements answer "is this tenant allowed to use this capability under the subscription?"

Examples:

- module access: invoicing, inventory, treasury, accounting, payroll, assets, projects, AI;
- feature access: multi-warehouse, bank reconciliation, advanced reports, API access;
- usage limits: users, documents, storage, tenants/branches, integrations, AI usage;
- support/service tiers where productized.

Entitlements must not:

- grant cross-tenant access;
- bypass RBAC/RLS;
- bypass fiscal/e-Fatura requirements;
- expose hidden modules to unauthorized users;
- delete tenant data when a limit is exceeded.

## Permission Boundary

[[Permissoes e Auditoria ERP]] remains the user-level security authority.

Flow:

1. tenant subscription grants the module/feature/limit;
2. tenant membership confirms the user belongs to that tenant;
3. role/permission confirms the user may perform the action;
4. service command enforces both entitlement and permission;
5. audit records the result.

This prevents a paid plan from becoming a security bypass.

## Billing And Treasury Boundary

SaaS subscriptions can generate billing records. [[Tesouraria ERP]] or an external payment provider handles collection.

Rules to preserve:

- recurring billing creates a billing run and line-level evidence;
- payment status should be derived from payment/treasury evidence, not manually edited access flags;
- overdue handling uses billing status plus grace policy;
- suspension affects access, not tenant data integrity;
- reactivation requires normalized billing state or explicit admin override.

## Fiscal Boundary

If NOVA-ERP invoices its own SaaS subscriptions to Cabo Verde customers, billing may need fiscal document issuance, IVA treatment and e-Fatura submission through [[Faturacao Eletronica]].

Until current fiscal design is explicit:

- `saas_invoices` should be treated as platform billing evidence, not automatically as legally issued fiscal documents;
- fiscal invoices for SaaS subscriptions should be generated through the same fiscal-document engine used by tenant business documents or a clearly separated platform-billing fiscal process;
- no production claim should be made about SaaS billing compliance without current Cabo Verde fiscal verification.

## Access Enforcement

The architecture source names two important service contracts:

- `saas_get_tenant_access`: returns effective tenant access for frontend and services.
- `saas_assert_limit`: enforces hard or soft usage limits.

Access enforcement should happen server-side for critical actions. UI gating is useful for UX, but it must not be the security boundary.

## Audit, Security And Tenancy

- Subscription records are tenant-scoped where they govern a tenant.
- Plan catalogs may be global/platform-owned.
- Subscription changes, entitlement overrides, price changes, billing runs, overdue transitions and suspensions require audit events.
- Enterprise overrides should require approval and reason.
- Suspension must not delete or corrupt tenant data.
- Platform admins and tenant admins need separate access boundaries.
- Billing/payment records may contain sensitive commercial data and should be permissioned.

## Cabo Verde Compliance Notes

The SaaS subscription layer itself is a platform business module. Compliance risk appears when NOVA-ERP bills Cabo Verde customers for its own service.

Current cautions:

- SaaS billing may require IVA and e-Fatura treatment.
- Platform billing must not be confused with tenant business invoices.
- If NOVA-ERP uses its own fiscal module to invoice subscriptions, platform-as-seller tenant/configuration must be explicitly modeled.
- Current legal/fiscal verification is required before production SaaS invoicing claims.

## MVP Acceptance Criteria

For the first sellable release, subscriptions are acceptable only if:

- tenant subscription state is explicit;
- plan/add-on entitlements are centrally evaluated;
- entitlements do not bypass user permissions or RLS;
- access enforcement happens server-side for critical limits;
- subscription lifecycle changes are auditable;
- overdue/suspension does not delete tenant data;
- billing runs are traceable to subscription items and periods;
- payment status is linked to treasury/external payment evidence or declared manual;
- fiscal SaaS invoicing scope is explicit: not implemented, internal billing-only or routed through fiscal module.

## Non-MVP Until Confirmed

- Proration.
- Downgrade scheduling rules.
- Coupons and campaigns.
- Automated payment gateway integrations.
- Complex approval workflows for enterprise exceptions.
- Customer self-service billing portal.
- Full fiscal SaaS invoicing without current Cabo Verde billing/e-Fatura design.
