# DNRE

## Snapshot

DNRE is the Cabo Verde tax authority context behind the invoice and electronic invoice materials in this wiki.

## Known Facts

- The source collection includes a DNRE/SITA invoice manual and DNRE e-Fatura technical manuals v10.0/v11.0.
  Source: [[2026-05-28 - Manual de Faturas em Cabo Verde]], [[2026-05-27 - Manual Tecnico da Fatura Eletronica v10.0]], [[2026-05-28 - Manual Tecnico da Fatura Eletronica v11.0]]
  Confidence: high

- The DNRE technical manual defines PE, DFE XML, IUD, document types, authorization, contingency, APIs and middleware behavior for the e-Fatura ecosystem.
  Source: [[2026-05-27 - Manual Tecnico da Fatura Eletronica v10.0]], [[2026-05-28 - Manual Tecnico da Fatura Eletronica v11.0]]
  Confidence: high

- The official e-Fatura manual page listed v11.0 as the latest technical manual on 2026-05-28.
  Source: [[2026-05-28 - Manual Tecnico da Fatura Eletronica v11.0]]
  Confidence: high

- The DNRE/SITA invoice manual maps invoice business rules such as issuance timing, required invoice elements, numbering, rectification, REMPE, State/public works invoicing, reverse charge, transport documents and archival, but it is explicitly orientative and non-normative.
  Source: [[2026-05-28 - Manual de Faturas em Cabo Verde]]
  Confidence: medium
